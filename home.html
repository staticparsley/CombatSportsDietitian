    About

    The home of modern evidence based nutrition science for fitness, performance, martial arts and fighting. Assessment based recommendations provided with scientifically proven results. Our focus is using the latest research to feel-able differences for recovery, energy and performance while optimizing body composition. Superior nutrition for whatever your fight is.

    Combat Sports Dietitian.com is a team of Registered Dietitians founded by John Sassone MS RD, a published researcher and author who is the only Brazilian jiu jitsu black belt and former pro MMA fighter licensed as a Registered Dietitian. While competing in Southern California as a bantamweight, John recognized a need for sport specific evidence based nutrition. Combat sports are uniquely demanding for athletes, with nutritional needs unparalleled in other sports
