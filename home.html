    The home of evidence based nutrition science for fighting

    The home of modern evidence based nutrition science for fitness, performance, martial arts and fighting. Assessment based recommendations provided with scientifically proven results. Our focus is using the latest research to providing significant differences for recovery, energy and performance while optimizing body composition. Superior nutrition for whatever your fight is.

    Combat Sports Dietitian.com is a team of Registered Dietitians founded by John Sassone M.S., R.D., a published researcher, author and the only registered dietitian in the world who is also a Brazilian jiu jitsu black belt and former professional MMA fighter While fighting out of Huntington Beach as a bantamweight on the Southern California pro circuit, John recognized a need for sport specific evidence based nutrition. John then completed a Master's of Science Degree in Nutrition with a focus on exercise physiology, graduating with a 4.0 GPA out of Cal State Long Beach. John has mastered every aspect of sports nutrition for combat sports.
